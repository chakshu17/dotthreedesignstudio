Design Studio

Our Philosophy

Dot Three Design Studio is guided by the philosophy of The Three Dots of Life: Mind, Body and Spirit that are interconnected to each other. These Three dots continually inform and redefine each other. Similarly, we believe that architecture is a tangible as well as phenomenological impulse that drives this interaction to encourage a higher sense of consciousness.

Our Goal

We as an avant-garde boutique firm aim to create enduring meaningful spaces which induce harmony, bliss and peace in the life of our customers. Our designs focus on implementing clean lines, minute details along with optimal use of natural light, ventilation and locally available natural building materials. More than just architecture, we endeavour to create a lifestyle for the occupants.

Our Methodology

As a collective studio we acquire architectural solutions through research followed by analysis & exploration of visual, spatial, formal, social and technological ideas. We are a customer oriented firm, meaning our spaces are primarily designed with exquisite minute details to cater the needs and contexts of our customers. Our designs imbibe a synergy of the traditional as well as modern elements to provide a stimulating experience of a space that is more permanent in our memory and includes our body as an integral part of the site.

Our Philosophy
We work as a collective studio, acquiring architectural solutions through study, analysis and exploration of visual, spatial, formal, social an technological ideas. As an extension of our mind , the spirit is also connected to the mind and body. All three constantly inform and redefine each other. Architecture is a physical and phenomenological entity and can participate in that interaction, encouraging a sense of spirituality or a higher level of consciousness.
Our goal lies in creating " enduring meaningful spaces " which induce harmony, happiness & peace in the lives of the users. We endeavour to create more than just architecture, a lifestyle for the occupants. Simple intelligent ideas such as optimum use of natural light, ventilation & locally available natural building materials and techniques have been our continuous effort.
We believe in creating architecture, which primarily responds to its users and context more than defining a signature style for us. We also believe that the beauty lies in detailing and it is our constant effort to work till the minute details, understanding the needs of the user.
Our designs imbibe a synergy of the traditional and the modern elements. Our designs are international and contemporary yet deeply rooted to the place and its context.
Sensual experience of a space gives the space a more permanent presence in our memory and includes our bodies as an integral part of the site .
Sensual linkages between the natural environment, built environment, and our own bodies creates a more complete understanding of the order of our world and the interactions within it, which in turn helps us to know ourselves.
