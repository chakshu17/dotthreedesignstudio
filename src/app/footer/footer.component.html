Dot Three Design Studio

Dot Three Design Studio is guided by the philosophy of The Three Dots of Life: Mind,
Body and Spirit that are interconnected to each other. These Three dots continually
inform and redefine each other. Similarly, we believe that architecture is a
tangible as well as phenomenological impulse that drives this interaction to
encourage a higher sense of consciousness.
